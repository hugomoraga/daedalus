# knowledge/

The organizational knowledge base — reference material the organization relies on to operate.

Distinct from `docs/` (foundational philosophy and model) and `specs/` (capability definitions). `knowledge/` holds the *operational reference*: glossaries, domain facts, external references, and the ubiquitous-language dictionary that keeps terms meaning one thing across all bounded contexts.

## What belongs here
- The ubiquitous-language glossary (shared vocabulary across contexts).
- Reference facts and external sources the organization depends on.
- Material that future knowledge-driven capabilities will consume.

## What does not belong here
- Governing rules (those are [`policies/`](../policies/) and the [Constitution](../memory/constitution.md)).
- Process definitions (those are [`workflows/`](../workflows/)).

*Grows organically across phases. The substrate for future knowledge-driven capabilities.*
